About CCA

Creative Collaboration Association

CCA (Creative Collaboration Association) is an officially recognized club of The Village School.

In the club, each member can create an activity that the club members can do as a whole. They can share it among the members and listen to their comments and suggestions. Then, we will do that proposed activity as a club. The purpose of the club is for the members to get out of their 'comfort zones' and socialize with various students from around the world.


We make. We share. We play.
Be creative! Members of the club will develop a creative activity that every member can enjoy. It could be a new board game, a new application of existing sports, an innovative way of community service, or anything that we all can enjoy. We may also hang out, socialize and just have fun with the club members after the activity.

Why You Should Join CCA

  • It's fun! We will do many exciting activities that will bond the club members as one.
  • Make friends! The club's primary purpose is to socialize among different variety of students. You will make some friends here.
  • Creative activities! Finding people to play activities that you want to play? Propose an activity, and we will play it!
  • Club trips! We will occasionally hold community service trips as a club, in cooperation with the Residential Life!
