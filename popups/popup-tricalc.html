Triangle Calculator


Triangle Calculator is a website that can calculate for the remaining sides and angles of a triangle. It can also calculate for the area of a triangle.

The calculator utilizes various trigonometry formulas: Law of Sines, Law of Cosines, Heron's Area Formula, and Oblique Triangle Formula.


⚠️  This app is not optimized for mobile devices.
